IT Hardware Storage Solutions

Working in IT can come with many challanges. One of which is keeping a we orginized stock room. While I was at Stone Plastics, this was no acception.

Many times, when a piece of equipment was needed, you would go up there and look for it to only come up blank or you would have to wade through a mountain of trash to get what you were looking for. To many of you, these images might look familiar in terms of your current IT storage solution.

IT Hardware Mess


My education background is in manufactured quality and chemistry. I always knew the IT storage closet was a bit insane where I worked. One day the regular IT guy went on vacation giving me the opportunity to to dive in to organization under the 5S methodology.

The 5S Methodology

  • Sort
  • Set in Order
  • Shine
  • Standardize
  • Sustain

I was told by one of the controls engineers that while I might spend some time organizing the IT storage closet, it wouldn't stay clean. It would have to be done peroidically. I don't think this is gonna be the case, and it is not because of the people I worked with. It is because the many times before when it was cleaned, people would not follow the last 2 steps.

The persons before me didn't standardize the area, and you can't sustain your sort or set in order without stanardization. Following months of work, these were the results.
